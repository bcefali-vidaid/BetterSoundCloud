Better SoundCloud

Notes on use:

  • By default no results are filtered
  • Playlists are not examined—meaning that the duration filter does not apply
  • A single song is typically less than 240 seconds (4 minutes)
  • Bug reports are appreciated

 

Filtration Criteria
In seconds, 0 = no minimum
In seconds, -1 = no maximum
Playlist tracks are not examined
Reposted playlists are covered by this setting
